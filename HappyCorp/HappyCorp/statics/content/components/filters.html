1. Hidden fields

The following fields can be hidden, those are the ones that will be serialized and sent to the back end to be processed.
You can add and remove the ones you don't need, this is just an example.
The current ones fit most of the projects.

Event
currentPagePiD
parentFolderPiD
categoriesPiD
authorPiD
tagsPiDs
queryString
queryString
orderBy
take
skip

This is the code for the hidden filters you should put in the body of your project.

        <div class="filters hide js-filtes-wrapper">
<input type="hidden" name="event" id="input_event" data-default="reloadResults" value="reloadResults" />
<input type="hidden" name="currentPagePiD" id="input_currentPagePiD" data-default="" value="" />
<input type="hidden" name="parentFolderPiD" id="input_parentFolderPiD" data-default="" value="" />
